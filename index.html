About Curved Sounds

Curved Sounds is created by Jay Curve, an ambient soundscape artist who wants to make music theory, songwriting and MIDI tools easy to access and free to use.

The goal is to help musicians, producers and creative learners explore chords, melodies, basslines, drums and new musical ideas without barriers.
